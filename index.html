About Me

Hi, I'm Roman, Front-End Developer from Odesa, Ukraine with over 13 years of experience in planning, coding, testing and maintaining websites and applications.

I am constantly reading, writing, and experimenting with the latest in web technologies.

With my extensive experience, I have worked on a wide range of projects, from small-scale websites to large-scale web applications. Worked with clients from the U.S., Norway, Germany, Austria on projects in Healthcare, Food, Fashion, Entertaiment, Games, Telecommunication, Sports business, Mass Media, Hospitality and other industries.

Each project I start with the deep analysis to define the best approach and choose the most appropriate tool set according to project specific goals and feature implementation.

I am able to work effectively in a team (have an experience as a Lead developer) or on my own. I pride myself on being able to use my knowledge and initiative to deliver high quality results to tight deadlines. I enjoy a challenge and look for the opportunity to expand and develop my skill set.
